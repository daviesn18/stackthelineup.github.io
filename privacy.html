Privacy Policy

Short version: your team's data stays on your device. No account, no ads, nothing sold.

Last updated: June 9, 2026

The short version

Stack the Lineup is a planning tool that runs on your iPhone and iPad. Your roster, lineups, position assignments, and game history are stored on your device. We do not require an account, we do not show ads, and we do not sell your personal information.

Information stored on your device

The information you enter to build lineups stays on your device (and in your personal iCloud backup, if you have device backups enabled):

  • Player names, jersey numbers, and position preferences you add
  • Batting orders, inning-by-inning position assignments, and game info such as date and opponent
  • Archived games and the season stats calculated from them
  • Any roster you import from a GameChanger CSV file

This information is not transmitted to us. We have no server that stores your roster or lineups.

Purchases

Stack the Lineup Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Apple handles the transaction and provides us only with the information needed to confirm that a purchase was made so Pro features can be unlocked. Apple's handling of payment data is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Diagnostics

If you have opted in to share analytics and crash data with developers in your device settings, Apple may provide us with anonymized, aggregated diagnostic reports to help us fix bugs and improve performance. These reports do not identify you and do not include your roster or lineup content. You can turn this off at any time in your device settings.

Children's privacy

Stack the Lineup is designed for coaches, who are adults. While the app helps you manage information about youth players, that information is entered by you, the coach, and is stored only on your device. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and we do not transmit player information off your device.

Third parties

We do not use third-party advertising networks or analytics SDKs that track you across apps. The only third party involved in normal use is Apple, for app distribution and in-app purchases.

Your control

Because your data lives on your device, you control it. Deleting a player, a game, or the app itself removes the associated data from your device. Restoring a Pro purchase on a new device does not transfer your roster; lineup data moves with your device backup, not through us.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top and post the revised version here. Material changes will be reflected in an app update where appropriate.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@stackthelineup.com.
