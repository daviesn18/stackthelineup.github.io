What Stack the Lineup is

Stack the Lineup is a planning tool. It helps you build a batting order, assign defensive positions inning by inning, flag fair-play issues based on rules you configure, track pitch counts you enter, and export PDFs for the dugout.

It is a coaching aid, not an official system of record. It does not referee your game, report to your league, or replace your league's rulebook or scorekeeping.

Fair play warnings and pitch counts are aids, not guarantees

The fair-play warnings, position checks, and pitch-count eligibility indicators are calculated from the rules you configure and the data you enter. They can only be as accurate as that setup. They may not match your league's actual rules, and they may be wrong if a setting, an inning count, or a pitch count is entered incorrectly or not at all.
