Getting started

Add your roster

On the Players tab you can add players one at a time, paste your whole roster at once, or import from a GameChanger CSV file. Each player gets a name and jersey number.

Set position preferences

Tap a player to tag positions as Strength, Capable, Emergency, or Never. This is what makes Auto-Fill put the right kids in the right spots. Position preferences are a Pro feature.

Build the lineup

On the Lineup tab, set the game date and opponent, then tap Edit and drag players to set your batting order. Your order carries over to the next game.

Fill the positions

On the Positions tab, tap the bolt to Auto-Fill. Choose a single inning or innings one through six. The app fills open spots while checking every fair-play rule. Review the Position Summary to see the whole game at a glance.

Bring it to the field

Export a PDF of your batting order and defensive grid to print or share. When the game ends, archive it to History so it counts toward your season stats.

Billing & Pro

Restore a purchase

Stack the Lineup Pro is a one-time $4.99 purchase tied to your Apple ID. If you reinstall the app or set up a new device, use the restore option on the Pro screen to unlock Pro again at no charge.

Refunds

Purchases and refunds for the App Store are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.

Still need help?

Email support@stackthelineup.com with your question and, if it's a bug, the device you're using and what you were doing when it happened. That helps get you a fix faster.
